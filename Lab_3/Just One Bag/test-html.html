Our Mission

Founded in August of 2012 and based in San Francisco, California, Just One Bag is a trusted community travel planner for people to discover, and book unique accommodations around the world - online or from a mobile phone.

Whether a last minute trip to Paris, or a last-minute reservation at Spagos, Just One Bag connects people to unique travel experiences, at any price point, in more than 34,000 cities and 190 countries. And with world-class customer service and a growing community of users, Just One Bag is the easiest way for people to monetize their extra space and showcase it to an audience of millions.
